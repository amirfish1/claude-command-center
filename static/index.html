🚗 Car Mode - drive your agents by voice
A hands-free voice operator for CCC. A dispatcher reads you your fleet, you say "take me into the ads session" to talk to that agent, then "back to the dashboard". Built for when you're away from the keyboard (driving, walking). Runs locally in a Terminal-style window.
Keys are stored locally in ~/.claude/command-center/car-mode.json (owner-only, never shown again). CCC also reads ccc-voice/.env if you prefer to manage them there.
What it costs
About $0.35/hr to run: routing agent on Claude Haiku 4.5 (~$0.10/hr), Deepgram speech-to-text (~$0.08-0.10/hr) and text-to-speech (~$0.15/hr). Near-free if you skip Deepgram and use your Mac's built-in speech. Your CCC sessions bill separately as usual.
If a key is missing - Car Mode degrades instead of breaking:
  • No Deepgram key → hands-free voice is off, but CCC's built-in browser mic and read-aloud (free, no key) still let you work by voice in the UI.
  • No Anthropic key → Car Mode can't start; it tells you what's needed instead of erroring.
